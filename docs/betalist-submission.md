## Description (~150 words)

Viaje360 is an AI travel companion that doesn't just plan your trip — it stays with you throughout the journey.

Tell it where you're going, and it generates a day-by-day itinerary with local restaurants, hidden gems, and must-see spots. But that's just the start.

**What makes it different:**
- 🧠 **Proactive briefings** — morning plans, evening recaps, weather alerts, and ticket reminders sent at the right time
- 🎒 **Smart packing list** — generated from your destination's weather, activities, and culture (power adapters, visa reminders, mosquito repellent)
- 💰 **Budget tracker** — set a budget, log expenses, get alerts when you're overspending
- 🗺️ **Local tips** — curated insider knowledge for 15+ cities (not AI-generated fluff)
- ✨ **Magic moments** — discovers hidden spots near your route at the perfect time of day
- 🎬 **Trip recap** — a Spotify Wrapped-style story of your journey

Free to try. Built for travelers who want less planning and more exploring.
